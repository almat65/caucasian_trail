About This Journey

Welcome to the Caucasian Trail documentation! This project tracks an incredible hiking route through the Caucasus Mountains, featuring mountain passes, river crossings, and breathtaking landscapes.

Trail Information

  • 📍 Multiple mountain passes ranging from 1208m to 3692m altitude
  • 🌊 Several river crossings (best crossed early morning)
  • ⚠️ Challenging sections requiring careful navigation
  • 🏞️ Stunning Caucasus mountain scenery
